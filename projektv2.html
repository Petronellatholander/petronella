I have created an innovative app design that promotes the recycling and reuse of secondhand perfumes. Through this project, as an innovation service, I have focused on quickly going from idea to product in just two weeks. My app design aims to enable users to sell the perfumes they no longer use, reducing waste and promoting sustainability. Through the app, users can easily list their perfumes for sale and browse through available products. I have likely included features that allow user reviews, payment integration, and searching for specific perfumes. My project is an excellent example of how rapid innovation services can realize creative ideas and address important societal issues like sustainability. By minimizing the time from idea to product, I have demonstrated how innovation can occur quickly and efficiently in this environment. My app design has the potential to promote a culture of recycling and reduce waste in the beauty industry.
